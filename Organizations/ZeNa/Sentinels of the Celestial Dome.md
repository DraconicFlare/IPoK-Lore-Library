The keepers of the [Temple of the Celestial Dome](Temple%20of%20the%20Celestial%20Dome), the largest temple to [Cōsh](Cōsh) outside of [Titan](Titan). Resides in the [Tower District](Tower%20District) of Zenith.

Currently led by [Hierarch Diam Goldscript](Hierarch%20Diam%20Goldscript), assisted by [Felix Rhodes](Felix%20Rhodes).
